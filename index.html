Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zombie Escape Online free to play?

Yes! Zombie Escape Online is completely free to play with no hidden costs, no required downloads, and no ads during gameplay.

Do I need to download or install anything?

No download required! Play instantly in your web browser on desktop or mobile. The game runs entirely online.

What themes are available in the game?

Choose from 15+ unique visual themes including: Neon Zombies, Pirate Adventure, Space Aliens, Dungeon Knights, Ocean Sharks, Kaiju Monsters, Spooky Forest, Indiana Explorer, Classic Arcade, Halloween Pumpkins, Winter Penguins, Jungle Safari, Food Frenzy, Retro Game Boy, and Battle Robots.

Can I play on mobile devices?

Yes! The game is fully optimized for mobile with touch controls and swipe gestures. Works on iPhone, iPad, Android phones and tablets.

How do I save my progress?

Click the LOGIN button and sign in with your Google account to automatically save your progress, highest level reached, and theme preferences across devices.

What type of game is this?

Zombie Escape Online is a turn-based tactical puzzle strategy game inspired by classic games like Daleks and Rodent's Revenge. Each move counts as you navigate grid-based levels, avoiding enemies that move twice as fast as you.

Are there different difficulty levels?

Yes! The game features progressive difficulty with increasingly challenging levels. Start with easy tutorial levels and work your way up to complex puzzles with multiple enemies and intricate maze layouts.

About Zombie Escape Online

Zombie Escape Online is a free turn-based puzzle strategy game playable directly in your browser. No download or installation required. Challenge your tactical thinking as you navigate grid-based levels, avoiding enemies while finding the perfect escape route.

Game Features

  • Turn-based strategic gameplay inspired by classic puzzle games like Daleks and Rodent's Revenge
  • 15+ unique visual themes: Zombies, Pirates, Space, Dungeons, Ocean, Kaiju, Forest, Halloween, Winter, Arcade, Retro and more
  • Progressive difficulty with increasingly challenging levels
  • No download required - play instantly in your web browser
  • Mobile and desktop friendly with touch controls and keyboard support
  • Save your progress with Google login
  • Free to play with no ads during gameplay

How to Play

Move your character one tile at a time using arrow keys or swipe controls. Enemies move two tiles toward you each turn. Use walls and strategic positioning to trap enemies or slip past them. Reach the exit to complete each level. Every move counts in this tactical puzzle game!

Available Themes

Customize your experience with multiple visual themes including: Neon Cyberpunk Zombies, Spooky Forest, Indiana Jones Adventure, Giant Kaiju Monster, Pirate Treasure Hunt, Jungle Safari, Space Aliens, Dungeon Knights, Classic Arcade, Ocean Sharks, Halloween Pumpkins, Winter Penguins, Food Frenzy, Retro Game Boy, and Battle Robots.
